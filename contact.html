Frequently Asked Questions

How do I watch anime for free?

Simply browse our library and click on any anime to start watching immediately. No account required.

Can I watch anime offline?

Currently, streaming is available online only. We are working on adding offline download support in the future.

Why is the video buffering?

Try lowering the video quality in the player settings. If the issue persists, check your internet connection.

How do I request a new anime?

Send us a message using the contact form above with the anime title. We review all requests regularly.
